About

Hi, I'm Catherine! I'm a full-stack developer based in Seattle, currently at Brown University studying computer science and applied math. At Brown, I am a member of the Women's Varsity Swim and Dive Team, an undergraduate TA for CSCI 0170 (an intro CS courses), and the Events chair of Girls Who Code.

I enjoy spending time in the kitchen making coffee and matcha at my home cafe, learning to cook traditional Chinese dishes from my mom, and baking elaborate desserts. I also love to get crafty - some favorite art forms are origami, collaging, and crocheting - but I'm open to try anything! One of my favorite activities is hosting cozy gatherings (such as home cafe pop-ups and casual dinner parties) to share all the things I love, so that others can enjoy them too.

In my free time, I like to get outdoors and soak up as much of the Seattle sun as I can. Catch me exploring local coffee shops and restaurants, thrifting, and hiking in the nearby mountains!
